Why Stryvr

Two problems, one platform.

The boards that serve people with real barriers to employment face the same two challenges. Stryvr was built for both.

01

Readiness is more than job skills

For someone re-entering the workforce, a single skills course rarely closes the gap — and a giant course list is its own kind of dead end. Stryvr organizes thousands of courses into subject-based pathways that span the whole journey: financial literacy, self-care and stability, foundational digital and AI skills, and credential-aligned training. Clients follow a clear path, not an overwhelming menu.

02

Outcomes you can't report if you lose contact

Your performance numbers depend on reaching participants 6 and 12 months after they exit — and that contact is the hardest thing to keep. Stryvr's mobile app keeps graduates engaged for years, so the line to them stays open when your reporting needs it most.
